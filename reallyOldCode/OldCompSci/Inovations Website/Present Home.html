Nanotechnology is used in many different fields. In sports, scientists use nanotechnology to create new materials for sportswear and better equipment. Scientists use nanotechnology to create better weapons and more durable, useful, protected, and helpful uniforms for the military. They are also using nanotechnology to try and find new cures and vacines. Nanotechnology is also being used to find newer, more effective and more efficient ways to create energy. They are researching and developing new and revolutionary devices and applications.
